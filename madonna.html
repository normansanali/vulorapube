Madonna Louise Ciccone aka Madonna is an American actress, singer, and songwriter. As of 2022, Madonna’s net worth is $850 million. She accumulated her net worth through her singing, songwriting, and acting career. She is in the industry since the year 1979 and majorly works with the genres of dance, electronica, and pop.

She is one of the most important personalities in popular culture and has been dubbed the “Queen of Pop” on many occasions. Additionally, she is renowned for pushing the limits of creative expression in popular music while retaining complete control over all aspects of her business.

Madonna Wiki/Biography

Born on 16 August 1958, Madonna’s age is 63 years as of 2022. She was born and brought up in a rich-class Catholic Christian family from Bay City, Michigan, United States.

She completed her early education at the Holy Family Regional School, North Campus Grades V3-5, and later at the Rochester Adams High School in Michigan, United States.

She later enrolled herself at the University of Michigan – School of Music, Theatre, & Dance in Michigan, the United States from where she completed her graduation. From the very beginning of her childhood, she was more interested in dancing, singing, and other co-curricular activities rather than studying.
