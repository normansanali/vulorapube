Angela Sarafyan – A Brief Overview

Angela Eleni Sarafyan was born on June 30, 1983, in Yerevan, (then) Armenian SSR, USSR. Her claim to fame is her portrayal of Clementine Pennyfeather in the acclaimed TV series “Westworld” on HBO.

Have you ever wondered how prosperous Angela Sarafyan is? As of early 2018, it has been estimated that her net worth stands impressively at $6 million, a sum she has amassed through her successful career that began in 2000.

Angela Sarafyan’s Financial Status

Angela’s roots trace back to Armenia, but she moved to the US with her family when she was just four years old. Her father, Grigor Sarafyan, is also an actor, and her mother is a painter. During her childhood, Angela received ballet lessons and learned to play the piano.

Her career kicked off with the role of Aisha Al-Jamal in the TV series “Judging Amy” in 2000. Subsequently, she made appearances in TV series such as “Buffy the Vampire Slayer” and TV films like “Paranormal Girl”. She continued to make guest appearances in various TV series until landing the lead role in the drama film “A Beautiful Life” in 2008. This was just the beginning of her successful journey that notably augmented her net worth.

In 2016, Angela was chosen for the pivotal role of Clementine Pennyfeather in the TV sci-fi mystery drama series “Westworld”, which earned her a SAG award nomination for Outstanding Performance by an Ensemble in a Drama Series. The list of her achievements doesn’t end there. She is currently involved in several projects, including a biopic about Ted Bundy titled “Extremely Wicked, Shockingly Evil and Vile”, and the lead role in the fantasy film “We Are Boats”, alongside Luke Hemsworth and Graham Greene.

Regarding her personal life, Angela was in a relationship with Rami Malek in 2012, but further details about her romantic life remain undisclosed to the public.
