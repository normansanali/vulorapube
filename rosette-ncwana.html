Young Famous & African Star Rosette Ncwana Wiki | Bio

Rosette Ncwana (age: 31 years; born on March 12, 1992) is a South African supermodel and actor based in Johannesburg. Rosette has been working in the modeling industry since 2012. She is one of the most famous faces among the African youth. Her popularity is visible from her vast internet fanbase.

These days Ncwana is hovering in the news because she recently became part of the Netflix reality show “Young, Famous & African,” season 2. The show’s second season landed on the OTT platform on May 19.

As many of you might already know, the second installment features old faces like Swanky Jerry, Diamond Platnumz, and 2Baba, including some new faces like Bonang Matheba, Luis Munana, and, of course, Rosette Ncwana.

What makes Rosette joining the show so special is the presence of her ex-partner, Andile Ncube, who has been part of the show since episode one. We do not want to give any spoilers, so watch it on Netflix, and if you want to learn more about Rose, keep reading.

How Old is Rosette Ncwana?

On March 12, 1992, Rosette Ncwana (age 31) was born in Midrand, Gauteng, South Africa. We do not know much about her father and siblings, but we do know that her mother’s name is Philodora Mogomotsi. Growing up in Midrand, Rose had a mediocre childhood but a passionate and creative mindset.

She was 16 when she decided to become a model, but she waited and completed her schooling first. After graduating from school, Rosette moved to Johannesburg for better opportunities. Moving to Jozi proved the right decision for Ncwana as she got to tap attractive chances to work with famous designers.

How Much is Rosette Ncwana’s Net Worth?

During the initial modeling days, Ncwana worked without an agency, but eventually, she felt the need and connected with Boss Models. After joining BM, Rose’s grew exponentially. From 2015 to 2018, she worked extensively with designers and brands.

She not only walked on the ramp but also appeared in print and digital commercials. Although we do not have much information, it is believed that Ncwana is also an excellent actress. You might have seen her in the popular game show “Deal or No Deal,” which aired in 2008.

In 2023, Rose landed a job in her second small screen production, “Young, Famous & African: Season 2,” where she shared the screen with her ex-husband Andile Ncube and famous African stars like Swanky Jerry, Nadia Nakai, Khanyi Mbau, and Diamond Platnumz.
