Ava Max

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, July 16, 2024

Ava Max is an Albanian and American singer and songwriter. Ava Max is well-known for her signature hairstyle, which has been dubbed the “Max Cut.” She is best known for her single Sweet but Psycho, which topped the charts in Sweden, Norway, and Finland.

Childhood and Early Life of Ava Max

Amanda Ava Koci, 27, was born on February 16, 1994, in Milwaukee, Wisconsin. She is of Albanian descent and holds US citizenship. Her father, Paul Koci, and mother, Andrea Koci, raised her. Her father was a pianist, and her mother was a professional opera singer.

Max also has an older brother, the identity of whom is unknown. Her parents moved to France from Albania in 1990, where they stayed for a year before obtaining American passports.

Education of Ava Max

She attended Lexington High School for a year and was regularly bullied. Furthermore, she began her singing career at a young age. When she was eight years old, she entered several Radio Disney singing contests at Greenbrier Mall in Chesapeake.

At the age of ten, she made her debut as an opening act at the NorVa in Norfolk, performing Whitney Houston’s 1987 single “I Wanna Dance with Somebody (Who Loves Me).”

Ava Max’s Professional career

Prior to signing with Atlantic Records, Ava starred in “Anyone But You,” a song co-written by Canadian record producer Cirkut and released in July 2016 on SoundCloud. She sang two different melodies on the Le Youth album “Clap Your Hands,” which was released on August 4, 2017. She released her debut single “My Way” on April 20, 2018, and it peaked at number 38 on the Romanian Airplay 100.

On May 11, 2018, Slippin, a collaboration with Gashi, debuted. On June 8, 2018, she appeared in the song Into Your Arms by American rapper Witt Lowry. Salt was released on SoundCloud about a month ago. On August 13, 2018, she released “Not Your Barbie Girl” as a promotional single.

Sweet but Psycho was released on August 17, 2018. The single was a global success for Max, reaching number one in over 22 countries, including Germany, Switzerland, Austria, Norway, Sweden, New Zealand, and the United Kingdom, where it stayed for four weeks.

She released her follow-up single “So Am I” on March 7, 2019, and it charted in the top ten in Poland, Norway, and Finland. On March 12, 2020, Max released “Kings & Queens” as the fifth single from her debut studio album Heaven & Hell. The song topped the Billboard Adult Top 40 chart on December 12, 2020. She was also a featured artist on the country song “On Me” by Thomas Rhett and Kane Brown.

It was released on May 15, 2020, as part of the soundtrack for the 2020 film Scoob!, along with a music video. The album’s first single, “Who’s Laughing Now,” was released on July 30, 2020, and the album’s second single, “OMG What’s Happening,” was released on September 3, 2020. “My Head & My Heart” was released as a bonus track from the streaming re-issue of Heaven & Hell on November 19, 2020.

Achievements of Ava Max

In 2019, she won Attitude Awards, MTV Europe Music Awards, and LOS40 Music Awards. In 2020, she won BreakTudo Awards, iHeartRadio Titanium Awards, and LOS40 Music Awards. In 2021, Ava won the Swiss Music Awards for Best Breaking Act International.
