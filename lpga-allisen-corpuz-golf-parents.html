More On Allisen Corpuz Wikipedia And Bio 

Allisen Corpuz was born and raised in Honolulu, Hawaii. from where she passed her high school at Punahou School.

As of the 2022 season, she finished T16 in the LPGA Q-Series to earn LPGA Tour membership for the 2022 season.
