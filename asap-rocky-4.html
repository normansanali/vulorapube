Career

ASAP Rocky kicked off his professional career in 2007. 

In 2011, he released two singles titled “Peso” and “Purple Swag”.

And also released his mixtape called “Live. Love. ASAP”.

Next year he signed a deal with the Sony/RCA of Polo Grounds Music. He has also collaborated with many guest artists like 2Chainz, Kendrick Lamar, and Santigold in his solo album “Live. Love. ASAP” which secured top number on the Billboard 200 chart”.

His singles “Goldie”, “Fashion Kila”, “Fuckin’ Problems”, “Wild For The Night” were highly appreciated and “Fuckin’ Problems” got 8th position on the Billboard Hot 100″.

In 2015 he released his next album titled “At.Long.Last.ASAP”, which also got top ranking on Billboard 200, and its single, “LSD” got positive responses from the viewers. His third album titled “Testing” was released in 2018 and ranked good on the Billboard 200.

Furthermore, he portrayed the character “Dom” in the 2015 comedy-drama film “Dope” and “William King” in the 2018 American legal drama film “Monster”. His other notable films and television projects include “Jeremy Scott: The People’s Designer”, “Zoolander 2”, “Popstar: Never Stop Never Stopping”, “Have a Good Trip: Adventures in Psychedelics”.
