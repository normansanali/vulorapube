Sarah Silverman (born December 1, 1970) is an American Actress, Producer, Stand-Up Comedian, and Writer from Bedford, New Hampshire. She started her career as a comedian and mainly considers topics like racism, religion, sexism, etc.

In fact, she also worked as a writer for the show. At the age of 17, she began her career as a standup comedian in Greenwich Village. In 1995, she became the cast member of Mr. Show with Bob and David.

The actress appeared in the movie Who’s the Caboose? as a lead role. In 2001, Silverman appeared on Late Night with Conan O’Brien. Later, she began her own show named “Jesus Is Magic”.

In addition, in 2007, Sarah cast in her new show titled “The Sarah Silverman Program“. Unfortunately, the show was later canceled by the Comedy Central after three seasons. Currently, the star is actively worked as a host called “I Love You, America“.

Furthermore, the Silverman and Kimmel split from each other. Later, she started dating “Michael Sheen” (American actor). But Sarah also broke up in 2017.
