Everything We Know About The Final Season Of 'Peaky Blinders'

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, September 13, 2024

Much to the delight of fans, Peaky Blinders, Steven Knight's British period crime drama series is back for its final season. Eager fans were left with a cliffhanger ending back in September 2019 and have been waiting with bated breath to find out the fate of Thomas Shelby, the tenacious gang leader. The all-powerful Shelby family has taken fans by storm, and the fast-paced plot and perfectly defined characters have created a global addiction like no other.

The fact that these are the final episodes of the Peaky Blinders has put the show in greater demand than ever before, and by the sounds of it, the final season is poised to deliver all the captivating drama, and thrilling, gruesome battles with rival gangs that fans have been craving. Steven Knight is calling this "the best series of all," and the final season is finally here, so settle in, and get ready for a thrilling adventure...."By Order Of The Peaky Blinders."

10 'Peaky Blinders' Final Season Begins With Tom Shelby's Life In The Balance

Fans have been left waiting and wondering about Thomas Shelby's fate, after being left with a cliffhanger ending at the conclusion of Season 5. The show left off with a very distraught and mentally unbalanced Thomas Shelby holding a gun to his head in an empty field. An image of his late wife, Grace and his beloved horse appeared before him, as Grace whispered, "The work's all done, Tommy. It's all done. We can walk away from all of this."

After the failed assassination of Alfie Solomons and a mysterious betrayal from within the Shelby family, Shelby has many reasons to pull the trigger on himself, and season 6 picks up in this exact moment, as his life dangles in the balance.

9 Season 6 Of 'Peaky Blinders' Is Going To Be Very, Very Dark

Previous series of Peaky Blinders have seen the characters led to some very dark places, and everything seems shrouded in mystery. Trust and loyalty are constantly being put the test, and brutal violence seems to lurk in every corner. Just when fans thought things couldn't get any more gut-wrenching for the Shelby family, the final season promises to be exceptionally dark. Cillian Murphy has stated the last few episodes will be very "gothic" "dark as F***" and "heavy," and fans are ready for it all.

8 Who's Missing From 'Peaky Blinders' Final Season?

Sadly, one of the key characters of the Peaky Blinders will be notably missing from the final season. The beloved Helen McCrory who played the role of Aunt Polly sadly lost her very private battle with breast cancer on April 16, 2021. Her absence has been felt on the show, and her death truly devastated her family, friends, and adoring fans. As a result of her failing health, McCrory was unable to film any episodes for season 6 and will be eternally missed. She leaves a void that can never be filled, and Cillian Murphy has lovingly dedicated this series to her.

7 Stephen Graham Joins The Cast Of 'Peaky Blinders'

Fans have been pleasantly surprised by the announcement that Help and Line of Duty star Stephen Graham will be joining the final season of Peaky Blinders. Knight is keeping Graham's role on the show top secret and has come forward to say that he hopes fans are "surprised" by the character that he plays. He is bound to thrill fans with his authentic native Liverpudlian accent, which has been featured in the newly released trailer for the show.

6 Gina Gray's Family Will Storm The Plot Line

Gina Gray's American family takes front and center in season 6, and sources have confirmed that this family will take on a prominent role in the development of the plot. The final moments of season 5 teased their potential links to Sir Oswald Mosley, casting shadows of doubt on Gina's true intentions, with confusion about where her loyalty really lies. The truth behind this family will soon rise to the surface.

.

5 The Snitch Will Be Revealed During 'Peaky Blinders' Final Season

Season 5 left fans wondering who the snitch was in the family. Thomas angrily shut down Michael's offer to run the business, Alfie Solomons was alive after what initially appeared to be the successful assassination of his character, and trust between family members was visibly broken. Thomas' mind was starting to unravel and the demons that have haunted him his entire life had become ferociously uncontrollable. Someone stood in the way of what seemed to be the perfect plan to assassinate Mosley, and fans will soon discover who that snitch was that sent the family careening into a state of shock and confusion.,

4 Tom Hardy's Epic Return To 'Peaky Blinders'

Tom Hardy quickly became a fan favorite on the show and he's making his presence known again in a big way for season 6. When he first emerged on the scene during season 2, as the leader of a London Jewish gang, he quickly became a frenemy to Thomas Shelby, and their rivalry ultimately ended with Thomas shooting him in the face.

Much to the shock of fans, the heavily scarred, yet very much alive Alfie Solomons made an epic return during the season 5 finale, and he claims to have started a new life in Margate, away from his past life of crime. Season 6 sees the reemergence of his character in London, suggesting the gang life may have drawn him back in.

3 Juicy Details About The Cast Of 'Peaky Blinders'

If it seems like everything on the Peaky Blinders is changing, fans can rest assured that some of the famous faces they're used to seeing in previous episodes will provide a much-needed nostalgic boost to season 6.

Of course, Cillian Murphy resurrects his iconic role as Thomas Shelby, Paul Anderson returns as Arthur Shelby, Finn Cole as Michael Gray, and Sophie Rundle promises to thrill fans as Ada Thorne. Sam Claflin plays the Fascist politician Sir Oswald Mosely, and as mentioned, Tom Hardy resumes his role as Alfie Solomons. Natasha O'Keeffe, Harry Kirton, Anya Taylor-Joy, and Kate Phillips also return.

New cast members include Conrad Khan, James Frecheville, Amber Anderson, Stephen Graham, and there remains the possibility of some high-profile guest appearances.

2 How To Watch 'Peaky Blinders' Final Season

Get ready for it, the premiere episode of this long-awaited, epic final season is upon us. Fans can resume their favorite addiction by tuning in to see the Peaky Blinders on BBC on Sunday, February 27th. The thrills begin at 9pm for those fortunate enough to be able to tap in to BBC to get their fix, but Netflix fans may have to wait a little while longer. There is no set release date for Peaky Blinders on Netflix, but it is presumed they will pick up the show within a few weeks.

1 'Peaky Blinders' Season 6's Big Surprise

Season 6 is already starting to feel bittersweet. There is undeniable excitement over the fact that season 6 is bringing fans the highly anticipated crime drama that they have waited so long for, yet the news is tainted by the realization that these are the final 6 episodes, and the Shelby family will soon disappear for good. Well, sort of.

Knight has confirmed that Peaky Blinders is going to hit the big screen with a movie already in the works. The huge transition from television to the big screen will include the return of the beloved main character Thomas Shelby, which will be played by Cillian Murphy. There have been rumors of multiple movies in the works, but for now, those that are already anxious about saying goodbye to this beloved series can take comfort in knowing there will be something more to look forward to.
