Firefly Lane's Greg Germann (Benedict) age, height, Instagram, roles

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

After waiting nearly two years, Firefly Lane season 2 was finally released on Dec. 2, 2022. For those who have already started watching the new episodes, you would have noticed a few new faces to the cast list. One new addition is Greg Germann who portrays Benedict Binswanger.

His character is described to have “some big secrets,” which, without giving away any spoilers for the new episodes, may be revealed during his campaign to become Governor this season.

If you’re curious about the new cast member or just want to learn more about Greg Germann, then you’re in the right place! Here’s everything you need to know about the Firefly Lane actor.

The Texas-born actor is 64 years old. He was born on February 26, 1958, meaning in the new year he will be celebrating his 65th birthday! His star sign is Pisces.

According to Celebrity Heights, the American actor stands at a height of 5 ft 10 inches tall.

If you’re looking to follow the actor on social media, you can start by finding him on Instagram under the handle @greg.germann. The Benedict actor currently boasts over 270,000 Instagram followers. If you give the actor a follow, you’ll find plenty of content to devour including pictures from his day-to-day life, updates from projects, and behind-the-scenes content.

With 119 acting credits to his name, according to IMDb, there’s no way you don’t recognize Greg Germann. He is probably best known for playing Richard Fish on the television series Ally McBeal, which earned him a Screen Actors Guild award, and as Dr. Thomas Koracick on the medical drama Grey’s Anatomy.
