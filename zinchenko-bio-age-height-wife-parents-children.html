Oleksandr Zinchenko Biography

Oleksandr Volodymyrovych Zinchenko is a professional Ukrainian footballer who plays in the English Premier League.

Zinchenko started his professional career in the Russian Premier League when he joined Ufa in 2014-2015. He made 33 appearances and scored only 2 goals after his two seasons at Ufa.

He then joined Manchester City in 2016-2017. Zinchenko spent 6 years at Manchester City which included a year loan at PSV. He made a total of 128 appearances and scored only 2 goals for Manchester City.

Zinchenko is currently with Arsenal. He joined the Gunners in 2022-2023 and has so far made 9 appearances for them.

How Old Is Oleksandr Zinchenko?

Zinchenko was born in Radomyshl which is in Ukraine. He was born on 15th December 1996 and is currently 26 years old.

What Is Oleksandr Zinchenko Height?

Zinchenko is 5ft 9in tall.

What Position Does Oleksandr Zinchenko Play?

Zinchenko is one of the versatile players that can play different positions. He can play as both Left-back and Left wing-back. He can sometimes operate as a midfielder too.

Oleksandr Zinchenko Career

Oleksandr Volodymyrovych Zinchenko has so far made a total of 193 appearances for his club sides having scored only 4 goals. He has managed to win the English Premier League four times, the FA Cup, the EFL Cup four times, and the FA Community Shield.

He first had an International call-up in 2015 and has since then been with the Ukrainian National Team making 52 appearances and scoring 8 goals.
Zinchenko won the Ukrainian Footballer of the Year in 2019.

Who Are Oleksandr Zinchenko Parents?
