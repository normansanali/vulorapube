James Brolin net worth: James Brolin is an American actor, producer, and director who has a net worth of $50 million. James Brolin made his net worth through his acting work in television and movies, but mostly through soap operas and sitcoms. He was born in Los Angeles, California and is currently married to singer Barbra Streisand and is the father of actor Josh Brolin. He is best known for his multiple on-screen roles, including those of television and films, as well as soap operas. Brolin was born in Los Angeles as Craig Bruderlin, but later changed his name to sound more appealing with his profession. After various unsuccessful roles, Brolin finally began to make headway in the 1970s. At 6’4″, he was able to take various leading roles that other actors were considered too short to portray. By the mid-80s, he had starred in a number of films and was almost cast as James Bond, but wasn’t given the opportunity when Roger Moore decided to continue his role. Brolin has been married three times, to Jane Cameron Agee, with whom he had two children, Josh, also an actor, and Jess. The couple divorced in 1984 after 18 years of marriage; after having been married 12 days after they met. Actress Jan Smithers became his second wife in 1985. The two had a daughter, Molly Elizabeth in 1987 and divorced in 1995. He has since been married to singer and actress Barbra Streisand since 1998.
