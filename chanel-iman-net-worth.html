Chanel Iman Net Worth

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, September 18, 2024

Chanel Iman has an estimated net worth of $8 million. She was born on December 1, 1990, in Atlanta, Georgia, USA. She has built her fortune primarily through her successful career in the fashion industry. Additionally, she has ventured into acting, showcasing her skills in the film “Dope” (2015).

Chanel has been immersed in the world of fashion since her early years, displaying an interest in the industry from a young age. She moved to Los Angeles with her family as a child and attended Fairfax High School. Her modeling journey began when she was 13, and she eventually signed with the Ford Modeling Agency in 2004.

Her career picked up momentum, and she graced the covers of renowned magazines and participated in various high-profile campaigns for well-known brands. In 2010, she became a “Victoria’s Secret Angel,” further boosting her net worth. Beyond her modeling career, Chanel has also ventured into entrepreneurship by opening her own boutique called “The Red Beg” in Culver City, California.

In addition to her professional endeavors, Chanel is also involved in humanitarian efforts, working with various organizations and campaigns to raise awareness and support causes such as the East African droughts.

Chanel’s personal life has also been in the spotlight as she has been in relationships with notable figures, including her current partner, basketball player Jordan Clarkson, and her previous relationship with rapper ASAP Rocky.
