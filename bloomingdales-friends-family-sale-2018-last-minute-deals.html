Will fall in full swing, there is no better time than the present to stock up on stylish pieces for the season, especially when they are a part of Bloomingdale’s Friends & Family Sale. If you’re like Us, we are taking advantage of the awesome deals to give our closet a fresh update. With tons of styles ranging from fur coats, cargo pants, dresses and more all up for grabs and 25 percent off, we are already making space in our wardrobe. Scroll down for our favorite picks!

Velvet by Graham & Spencer Top Dolman

Who doesn’t love to show off some skin? This dolman top is the perfect way to make a flirty statement. Crafted with a wide boatneck and three-quarter sleeves, our closet is in for a stylish spin. A great pairing with skinny jeans and booties for a fall-approved look, we can’t wait to flaunt this sassy number. Available in an ethereal white and black hue, this garment will mix and match with our closet seamlessly.

See It: Grab the Velvet by Graham & Spencer Top Dolman at Bloomingdale’s for 25 percent off the original price of $80, now $60.

AllSaints Balfern Leather Biker Jacket

Leather jackets are one of our favorite ways to complete our looks and this pick has Us completely smitten! Combining the best of both worlds, moto detailing and trendy flair, this design exudes biker babe vibes. Dressed up or down, we can count on making a bold statement every time we step out.

See It: Grab the AllSaints Balfern Leather Biker Jacket at Bloomingdale’s for 25 percent off the original price of $498, now $373.50.

Aqua Luxe Faux Fur Coat

Beat the cold weather in style with this vibrant faux fur coat! Crafted with side slit pockets, you can house all of your small essentials with ease. Its oversized notch lapels and long sleeves give this topper a trendy, yet chic flair. This plush find is great for wearing with casual ensembles and beyond.

See It: Grab the Aqua Luxe Faux Fur Coat at Bloomingdale’s for 25 percent off the original price of $168, now $126.

Stuart Weitzman Women’s Slipknot Suede Loafers

These suede loafers are the epitome of sophistication. Designed with a square apron toe and slip-on style, this shoe will keep your feet comfy all day long. Its tassel and hardware detailing are luxe added touches. Available in a sleek black hue and a gorgeous dovetail shade, you can bet that we are adding both colors to our closet.

See It: Grab the Stuart Weitzman Women’s Slipknot Suede Loafers at Bloomingdale’s for 25 percent off the original price of $425, now $318.75.

Sanctuary Cargo Jogger Pants

Cargo pants, anyone? An undeniable staple, this design is a must-have for every fashionista. The bottoms feature a drawstring waist and elasticized cuffs, as well as ankle zips (an edgy factor that we love). The pants are available in green and black. The perfect match with a loose tank, leather jacket and booties, we can’t wait to step out in style.

See It: Grab the Sanctuary Cargo Jogger Pants at Bloomingdale’s for 25 percent off the original price of $119, now $89.25.
