Jennifer Lopez Looks So Elegant in Backless Low-Cut Sundress

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

Jennifer Lopez is back with another summer mood board-worthy street style look.

Yesterday, the singer stepped out in Los Angeles wearing a backless black sundress with an airy skirt and a tight bodice, which also featured a halter neckline and a deep-V cut. She paired the dress with minimal accessories, opting for black heels, stud earrings, and a brown tote bag.

She kept her glam equally minimalistic, going for her signature nude lipstick and tying her hair up in a sleek high ponytail.

Bellocqimages/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

Bellocqimages/Bauer-Griffin//Getty Images

Earlier in the week, J.Lo was spotted wearing another all-black summer look while visiting fiancé Ben Affleck on the set of his forthcoming movie, an untitled project that follows a Nike marketing executive's journey to persuade Michael Jordan to represent the brand.

At the time, the Marry Me actress wore a black jumpsuit that cinched at her waist with a drawstring. She paired the outfit with strappy black platform espadrille heels, pulled from her JLO Jennifer Lopez shoe line, plus minimal jewelry and tinted sunglasses.

The multi-hyphenate opened up about how she navigates her career and her personal life during a recent appearance on Good Morning America.

"I love my career, but nothing is more fulfilling to me than being able to build my family with someone who I love deeply and is just as dedicated to family and to each other as we can be," she said. "I love the idea of the future and what we can create, but I really want to savor the moment."
