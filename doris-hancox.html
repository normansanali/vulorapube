Doris’s Facebook page indicates that she works for Los Angeles County in California. Doris, on the other hand, had other plans for her life.

Doris did get her chance to act, albeit a little late. In 2008, she appeared in the short film, Common Bond. She also appeared in the film The Truth in 2010. She made an appearance on an episode of the TV show Black-ish in 2017.

Aside from her acting roles, she had appeared as herself in several reality shows. In fact, she had an intriguing story about her first game show. In 2015, she appeared on Celebrity Family Feud with her son, Anthony Anderson.

When asked on the show, “Name something a new magician might pull a rabbit out of,” she replied, “his nuts.” Doris apparently misheard “new” as “nude.” The host, Steve Harvey, was taken aback by her response, which embarrassed her son.

Later, the show’s producers approached Anderson to discuss his mother. Anderson quickly apologized. However, they intended to bring her to To Tell You the Truth. They were blown away by her personality.

Doris did appear on To Tell You the Truth, and she is still on the show after five years and 61 episodes. Fans of the show referred to her as Mama Doris. She was a fan favorite on the show. She was absent for a few episodes in 2021. People were concerned that she had left the show. She did make a quick return to the show. She did not, however, explain why she was absent from the show.

Access Hollywood, Good Morning America, Jimmy Kimmel Live!, The Talk, Access Daily, Animal Nation with Anthony Anderson, Extra with Billy Bush, Entertainment Tonight, and others are among her other reality TV credits.
