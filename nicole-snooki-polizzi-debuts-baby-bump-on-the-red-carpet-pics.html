Nicole Snooki Polizzi Debuts Baby Bump on the Red Carpet: Pics

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Wednesday, September 4, 2024

Dressing for two! Nicole “Snooki” Polizzi debuted her baby bump on the red carpet at The Cut’s How I Get It Done event on Monday, March 4.

The Jersey Shore: Family Vacation star, 31, sported a white top that hugged her budding belly, as well as a navy cardigan, black skinny jeans and leopard-print boots.

The Snooki Shop owner showed off the chic look on Instagram before the event with a mini photo shoot in her kitchen, courtesy of her 4-year-old daughter, Giovanna. “Hay Mawma,” Polizzi captioned the three pics cradling her bump.

Polizzi, who also shares 6-year-old son Lorenzo with her husband, Jionni LaValle, revealed in November 2018 that she is pregnant with their third child.

“What I’m thankful for this Thanksgiving,” the reality star wrote at the time. In the Instagram announcement, her son and daughter looked down at a framed ultrasound photo.

The Strong Is the New Sexy author went on to reveal that she and LaValle, also 31, are expecting a baby boy in May. She told Us Weekly exclusively how her husband felt about the news.

“It’s the third kid. He was like, ‘Oh, s–t. Here we go,’” Polizzi said in January, although she admitted he’d been “waiting” for her to get pregnant.
