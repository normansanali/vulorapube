Know About Demetrius Haley, Who was Involved In The Killing of Tyre Nichols

Demetrius Haley (born on January 4, 1993) is a 30-year-old former Police officer who served for 3 years at MPD (Memphis Police Department). In January 2023, he got fired alongside 4 other cops for killing an innocent motorist named Tyre Nichols.

As soon as the concerned authorities learned about the incident, an internal investigation was conducted, which revealed that 5 policemen named, Desmond Mills, Emmitt Martin iii Justin Smith, and Tadarrius Bean, including Haley, harmed Nichols using undue force and killed him.

On January 10, following the demise of Tyre’s death, people started demanding the public release of the bodycam recording, but authorities delayed the release and waited for the matter to cool down.

Eventually, on January 27, 2023, Tyre Nichols’s cam footage came forward and shook everybody because we got to see how a group of men under the influence of power and authority committed an unforgivable deed.

It has not been much longer since George Floyd was murdered, and now we have got to witness one more such incident; the only difference this time is that the killers are African-Americans and not Caucasians, which makes this a case of police brutality.

Why did the Cops Pull Over Tyre Nichols?

It started on January 7, 2023, when MPD officers stopped Nichols for alleged rash driving; the cops showcased irrational behavior that made Tyre believe that they were part of a local gang that impersonates policemen and steals cars. Somehow the young father escaped, and the chase started.

Soon law enforcers caught Tyre and made him lie on the ground using unnecessary force, tasers, and pepper spray.

Nichols kept asking what his fault was, but Demetrius and his crew did not listen to anything. The bruised Nichols felt uncomfortable breathing, after which emergency services took him to a nearby hospital, where he died 3 days later.

Bodycam Footage of Police Officers in Tyre Nichols Case

20 days after the incident, Memphis Police Department released a video showing what happened with Nichols. The police officers in the video can be viewed using excessive and unnecessary force against the young motorist.

Tyre was cooperating, but the officers were using abusive language and extreme power, including tasers and pepper spray; one can be seen kicking brutally.

After several minutes of facing the wrath of the Memphis law enforcement unit, Tyre’s health started deteriorating. He could not even sit straight and fought for his life in the hospital for three days, but sadly luck was not on his side, and he passed away, leaving behind a little child.

From Where is Demetrius Haley?

According to sources, Demetrius Haley (age 30) was born on January 4, 1993, in Memphis, Tennessee, United States. He spent his life in places like Cordova. The information that has come forward revealed that Haley had a wonderful childhood; he received a good education. In 2011 he finished schooling and joined college, where he studied law.

After graduating with a bachelor’s degree, he returned to Memphis, where he decided to join the police. He started with Shelby County Division of Corrections, during which he faced charges of allegedly strip-searching an inmate named Cordarlrius Sledge but somehow managed to come out clean. In August 2020, MPD hired him offering him a handsome salary of $45,623.

It seems like, over time, he forgot that he go the job to help people, not abuse them, because no one with a straight mind could do what he did with Tyre Nichols.
