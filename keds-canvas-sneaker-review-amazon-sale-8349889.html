Sneakers go with everything, from a long-sleeved maxi dress to a pair of mom jeans with a cozy oversized cardigan, an example of their ease of styling and year-round appeal. That's why I'm buying multiples of these timeless transitional sneakers that I’ve been wearing for years, aka Keds Champion Canvas Sneakers, while they are on sale for $20 during Amazon's Prime Big Deal Days. 

Keds Champion Canvas Sneakers

I've been a loyal fan of these sneakers for nearly two decades; my last pair lasted eight years and I only tossed them after I couldn't fit into them anymore — they stand the test of time even with daily wear.
