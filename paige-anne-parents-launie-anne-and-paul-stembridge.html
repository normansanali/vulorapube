Launie Anne And Paul Stembridge?

Posted by Martina Birk on Sunday, July 14, 2024

When the April 17th episode of the American Idol Season 21 aired, something strange happened as host Ryan Seacrest announced that one of the Top 26 Finalist contestants had dropped out of the competition but did not bother to reveal his/her name. Seacrest, instead, revealed that ex-Season 21 contestant Paige Anne would fill in the void.

Find out about Paige Anne’s parents in the Wiki article below.

American Idol: Who Are Paige Anne Parents?

Although Ryan did not disclose the identity of the contestant who dropped out of the competition, singer Beckett McDowell confirmed through an Instagram story that he had made it to the Top 26. Speculations suggesting that Beckett was the one to quit the show began Sunday (April 15) when the show’s official Instagram shared a video of the top 26, which seemed to feature Beckett.

Paige Anne, who was defeated by Megan Danielle at the end of Hollywood Week by a small margin, became the “first alternate” to rejoin the show. “I said goodbye to ‘Idol’ and everything. Then I got invited back. Everything happens for a reason,” Paige told.

Paige made a grand return to the popular ABC show with an impressive rendition of Miley Cyrus’ “Wrecking Ball”–a performance which made even judges feel that they’d made a mistake not letting Paige enter the Top 26 in first place.

Talking more about Paige Anne’s musical career, she has been a featured singer in the country-rock cover band, Almost Famous. At 16, she already has experience headlining national anthems at the Monster Energy SuperCross, Lucas Pro Oil MotorCross, Utah Jazz basketball games, Arizona Diamondbacks baseball games, and the Idaho State Fair.

Meet Launie Anne, Paige Anne Mother

Paige’s mother, Launie Anne, is as supportive as a mother can be. After the return of her daughter to the Top 26 Finalist of American Idol Season 21, Launie took to Instagram to write, “My baby girl is back! American Idol asked her back to be a part of the #Top26. I am asking for every person to vote for my Paige Anne. She has worked so hard and to be given this 2nd chance is an open door!”

After Paige’s 16th birthday in September 2022, Launie posted a picture of herself and her daughter to her Instagram and wrote, “My newest 16-year-old….and taller than her mama…this is a weird feeling… .”

More about Launie, her parents are Kenneth Leon Tuckett (father) and Cindy Tuckett (mother). Unfortunately, her father passed away on April 9, 2008, at the age of 53 after a long battle with Primary AL Amyloidosis. Launie’s siblings include two brothers, Dustin, and Eric Tuckett.

  • Launie Anne Age

As per radaris.com, Launie Anne turned 44 years old in 2023. She celebrates her birthday every 20th of April.

  • Launie Anne Job

Paige’s mother Launie handles multiple jobs at one time. Her first job is as Chief of Operations at White Pine Charter School, where she has been working since April 2021. Her second job is as Owner and CEO at Different Property Management (since June 2011), where she manages rentals and Airbnb for her clients.

Launie’s third job and probably the biggest of all is her work as a realtor at Keller Williams Realty East Idaho, where she began working in August 2007. She specializes in all areas of Hard assets, including, Property management, Airbnb, Investing, and Construction, and helps people buy and sell their homes.

Launie formerly worked as a realtor at WinStar Realty.

  • Is Launie Anne On Instagram?

You can find Launie Anne on Instagram at @launielou where she has almost 700 followers. Launie also has over 2.3 thousand friends on Facebook, where she goes by her own name.

Meet Paul Stembridge, Paige Anne Father

Paige Anne’s father, Paul Stembridge, is currently in Hollywood cheering his daughter on the grandest stage of American Idol. On April 19, 2023, he shared, “In Hollywood, CA for American Idol with Paige Anne and Nick Stembridge at Lucifer’s Pizza. I love these two! Go Team PAIGE!.” Paul also asked his followers on Facebook to vote for his daughter Paige Ann.

On September 19, 2022, Paul wished his little girl a happy 16th birthday.

  • Paul Stembridge Age

Born in July 1973, Paul’s age was 49 at the time of this writing.

  • Paul Stembridge Job

Idaho Falls native, Paul Stembridge, owns Stembridge Holdings, LLC. Since August 2021, he has also been working as a counselor at Compass Academy.

Previously, Paul has worked as a government instructor, business teacher, and school counselor in Terreton, Idaho for the last 12 years.

  • Is Paul Stembridge On Instagram?

No, Paul Stembridge is not on Instagram. You can find him only on Facebook.

 Related FAQs

  • Are Paige Anne Parents Still Married?

Unfortunately, Paige Anne’s parents are no longer married to each other. After their separation, Paige’s mother, Launie, remarried a man named Colby Shelman on May 14, 2010.

Likewise, Paige’s father, Paul Stembridges, also remarried Megan.

  • Where Do Paige Anne Parents Reside?

Although separated, Paige’s parents, Paul and Launie, still live in the same place, that is Idaho Falls, Idaho.

  • How Many Kids Do Paige Anne Parents Have?

Paige Anne’s parents had only two kids from their relationship. Besides Paige, they had a son named Nick.

Launie has three more kids, a son named Jack and two daughters, from her marriage with Colby Shelman. Launie’s son, Jack, turned 9 years old in September 2022.

Paul and his wife Megan also welcomed twins in 2015.
