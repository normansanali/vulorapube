Ki Hong Lee Net Worth

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, September 26, 2024

Ki Hong Lee:

$4 million

Ki Hong Lee is a Korean-American actor. He started his acting career in the 2010s, first performing in short films. He next made his film debut in Yellow Face. His breakthrough came shortly after landing the role of Minho in the movie The Maze Runner. Subsequently, he acted in the films The Stanford Prison Experiment and Everything Before Us. He next reprised his role as Minho in the sequel Maze Runner: The Scorch Trials. This year, he had roles in the feature films The Mayor and Wish Upon. He also star in the upcoming sequel Maze Runner: The Death Cure, to be released in 2018.
