Here's Why Amber Heard Called Johnny Depp's Friends &quot;Old And Boring&quot;

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, September 7, 2024

The trial between Johnny Depp and Amber Heard seems like a movie plot. Fans are talking about the court case none stop, with stories coming such Heard getting upset with Depp's "Winona Forever" tattoo, or Johnny's savage comebacks against Heard's lawyer. Her apparent words caused Depp to smirk in court. Let's take a look.

What Did Amber Heard Say About Johnny Depp's Friends?

This time around, things are a little bit different, as Amber Heard is the one facing a lawsuit in court. According to Depp and his team, Heard's words almost destroyed Johnny Depp's professional career as he missed out on several roles and lost out on even more during that span.

Johnny Depp's team issued a statement against Heard, stating alongside Rolling Stone that Depp must be compensated for the damage done to his career and mental health.

“Johnny Depp is a victim of abuse. He has been since Ms. Heard made her very first false allegation in 2016, and has continued to be subject to abuse ever since, as outlined in his testimony."

"Today’s cross-examination by Ms. Heard’s attorney has proven to be no exception. Mr. Rottenborn’s aggressive and, coincidentally, ‘jack-hammer’ approach toward a man who has suffered extensively over the last six years proves that the mistreatment of Johnny goes well beyond Ms. Heard’s grievous behavior. Colorful texts, which Johnny has already apologized for, do not equate to physical actions and Mr. Rottenborn still has not been able to connect the two, nor will he ever be able to.”

Fans are all over the case, with revealing information about the two coming out, including how Heard really felt about Johnny's friends.

Amber Heard Did Not Enjoy Spending Time With Depp's Entourage

There is a lot of information exposed about Depp and Heard's personal lives. In fact, Depp's sister told a story about Heard and how she called Johnny an "old, fat man," when he revealed that Dior was interested in working with him.

It seems like the abuse continued when this piece of information was revealed in the courtroom. It was said that Heard had no interest in spending time with Depp's entourage, given this reason.

"She told me she didn't like hanging around at his house with his friends because it was boring and it was all old men playing guitars and it wasn't interesting her."

Fans clearly have chosen a side, and they aren't shying away from what they feel is the truth in their opinion in regard to the case.
