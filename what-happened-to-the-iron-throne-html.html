Heres What Happened to the Iron Throne

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

For eight seasons, we’ve been wondering who’s going to end up on Game of Thrones’ Iron Throne, assuming the seat itself would still be around. Sunday night’s series finale had a little surprise: It melted! After Jon Snow stabbed Daenerys Targaryen, Drogon — the last remaining dragon — threw a tantrum and melted the throne that led his mother to her demise. (A fitting end: “The breath of the greatest dragon forged the Iron Throne. The swords of the vanquished, a thousand of them, melted together like so many candles,” said Viserys Targaryen of the fancy chair in season one.) Here is the Iron Throne’s death. RIP:
