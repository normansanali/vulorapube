Vikrant pushed Kirti off from cliff

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

Keh Doon Tumhein episode starts with Ritu locking Kirti’s house gate from outside. Puru insists Kirti to buy ice cream for him. Kirti refused as he have already drink cold mango shake. Puru gets stubborn and says if she won’t buy him ice cream for him, he will buy himself as he have money to buy ice cream. Kirti asks him to not be stubborn. She goes to kitchen to take plate on Madhuri asking for it. Puru goes out alone and finds the gate locked. Ritu says whoever will try to come between her and Vikrant will have to pay for it.

RITU’S VILE MOVE, VIKRANT SAVED PURU

Puru started to go back. Only then, Ritu sees a truck coming so open the gate again. Finding gate open Puru goes on road. Vikrant witness it through his window and decides to take advantage of Ritu’s desparate nature. Truck was about to hit Puru. Vikrant saved him and Kirti comes out and ask him if he is fine. Puru assures her. Kirti scolds him for not obeying her saying. Kirti thanks Vikrant to save Puru, then noticed bruises on his hand. She offers to treat him. Vikrant says it’s nothing big. Kirti says she’ll feel bad if she don’t treat him. Ritu feels bad that Vikrant got hurt, cause of her.

VIKRANT REMEMBERS HIS MOTHER IN KIRTI

Kirti treats him. Later, She goes to his home taking tiffin for him. She insists to serve him and starts humming “Lag Jaa Gale” song while doing it. Vikrant remembers his mother singing the same song and got emotional. He asks Kirti to continue when she stops. Kirti asks him to not make fun of her. Vikrant says talent shouldn’t be hidden. Kirti tells about mishaps she do daily indicating she don’t have any talent. Vikrant says a poem of Malhar for Kirti and Kirti joins him. Kirti got happy knowing Vikrant has same interest as her.

VIKRANT DETERMINES TO KILL KIRTI

Vikrant asks her when she’ll go back to Bhopal. Kirti says she don’t have any plan to go back until she solve Anju’s mystery and catch her boyfriend. Vikrant asks do she have believe that Anju’s boyfriend is guilty and she’ll find Anju. Kirti says she definitely achieve what she decides to do. Vikrant thinks in mind to not be emotional after Kirti singing his mother song and decides to kill her before she catches him.

ANJU’S PHONE FOUND, THE SCHOOL TRIP

Police finds Anju’s phone from ooty as it was in truck which was taking stuff Panchghati to Ooty. Vikrant asks Kirti and Puru to their school trip. Vikrant convinces Kirti to come after her initial denial. Kirti goes to shopping with Madhuri and Neha where she got to know about Neha’s kleptomania disease. Next day, she, Puru and Vikrant goes to school trip. She asks him why he didn’t marry yet. Vikrant says he didn’t find any girl who can accept him the way he is. He says he don’t like if anyone tries to change him.

VIKRANT PUSHES KIRTI OFF OF CLIFF

On picnic spot, Vikrant took Kirti to a cliff. Kirti liked the view and goes close to edge. Vikrant asks her to not go. Kirti says she is not scared of anything. Vikrant thinks it’s her mistake and pushes her off the cliff. Episode Ends.
