Dwayne Johnson's Daughter Simone Officially Signs with WWE and Has Started Training at the Florida P

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, July 20, 2024

Dwayne Johnson's 18-year-old daughter Simone is making it clear that she wants to follow in his footsteps, as she officially signs with the WWE and begins training at the WWE Performance Center.

Simone Johnson is the daughter of 10-time WWE world champion, Dwayne "The Rock" Johnson, and ex-wife Dany Garcia. Now that she's of legal age and expressed her intent to be a wrestler, she's begun training at the WWE Performance Center in Orlando, Florida.

Prior to her training in Orlando, Florida, Simone was reported to be an undergraduate student at New York University.

Dwyane Johnson and his family at the premiere of "Central Intelligence" in 2016 | Source: Getty Images/GlobalImagesUkraine

BECOMING THE FIRST-EVER FOURTH GENERATION WWE SUPERSTAR

The 18-year-old will become the first-ever fourth-generation superstar in WWE history, following her great-grandfather, "High Chief" Peter Maivia, her grandfather Rocky Johnson, and of course, her father.

Simone aims to carry on the legacy left by her family in the WWE and is excited to do so. During an interview, she revealed:

"It means the world to me. To know that my family has such a personal connection to wrestling is really special to me and I feel grateful to have the opportunity, not only to wrestle, but to carry on that legacy."

A DREAM COME TRUE FOR SIMONE

After news of the beginning of her wrestling career hit the internet, she decided to confirm it herself by sharing a picture of her smiling in a WWE t-shirt. She dedicated her caption to her younger self, the version of her who first fell in love with the sport.

"To the little girl who fell in love with wrestling & said "this will be my life one day," this is for you. I'm humbled, grateful & ready to work. Let's do this."

SIMONE'S EDUCATION BACKGROUND

Prior to her training in Orlando, Florida, Simone was reported to be an undergraduate student at New York University. Previously, she graduated from NSU University School in Fort Lauderdale, Florida. It is unclear whether or not she's decided to set aside her college education to focus on wrestling.

Dwayne Johnson shares Simone with his ex-wife Dany Garcia. He recently married longtime partner Lauren Hashian at a private Hawaii ceremony last August, and they share two daughters, Jasmine Lia, and Tiana Gia, together.
