Foleys Status For Raw Next Week, Rougeau Health Update

Posted by Martina Birk on Wednesday, July 31, 2024

Foley's Status For Next Week's Raw

According to several readers who purchased tickets for next Monday night's Raw show, which is being taped in Hershey, PA, Mick Foley is being advertised for a special appearance, along with a dark match main event of John Cena, Randy Orton, and CM Punk vs. The Miz, R-Truth and Mark Henry.

Jacques Rougeau Health Update

According to a new report by The Wrestling Observer, doctors have told former WWE star Jacques Rougeau that they are 99% certain he does not have prostate cancer. Rougeau will resume testing on November 21, and if all goes well he will require a small surgery to deflate his prostate.
