Reshiram &amp; Zekrom-GX (Cosmic Eclipse 157)

Posted by Martina Birk on Monday, July 29, 2024

From Bulbapedia, the community-driven Pokémon encyclopedia.

Reshiram & Zekrom (Japanese: レシラム＆ゼクロムGX Reshiram & ZekromGX) is a Dragon-type Basic TAG TEAM Pokémon-GX card. It is part of the Cosmic Eclipse expansion.

Card text

Fabled Flarebolts
らいえんむそう		90×
Discard up to 3 in any combination of basic and basic Energy cards from your Benched Pokémon. This attack does 90 damage for each card you discarded in this way.
This attack does 170 damage to 1 of your opponent's Benched Pokémon. If you played N's Resolve from your hand during this turn, this attack also does 170 damage to 1 of your opponent's other Benched Pokémon. (Don't apply Weakness and Resistance for Benched Pokémon.) (You can't use more than 1 GX attack in a game.)

Release information

This card was included as a Regular card, a Full Art card, and as a Secret Rainbow Rare in the Cosmic Eclipse expansion, first released in the Japanese Dream League subset, each with artwork by Naoki Saito.

Gallery

Trivia

  • This card and Solgaleo & Lunala-GX are the only TAG TEAM cards in which an effect of their GX attack will not activate unless a specific supporter was played beforehand, instead of having a sufficient amount of additional energy cards attached.
  • This card is the only TAG TEAM where both members have been featured on other TAG TEAM cards: Pikachu & Zekrom-GX and Reshiram & Charizard-GX.

Origin

N, who is featured in each print's illustration, summons Reshiram or Zekrom in Pokémon White and Pokémon Black, respectively, in the final battle with him. The Fabled Flarebolts attack references the Fabled Cave, a Mirage spot in Pokémon Omega Ruby and Alpha Sapphire where Reshiram and Zekrom can be caught.
