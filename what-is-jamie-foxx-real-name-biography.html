What is Jamie Foxx real name biography?

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Tuesday, July 9, 2024

Jamie Foxx is an American actor, comedian, and singer. Jamie Foxx’s real name is Eric Marlon Bishop. He is a graduate of Terrell High School and Alma mater of  United States International University. Foxx was adopted by his maternal grandparents after his parent separated.

At age five Foxx has started learning how to play the piano, this developed his interest in music. He later had the scholarship to study at the United States International University (later Alliant International University) in San Diego to study classical piano and music theory.

Foxx is noted for acting in movies such as Jarhead (2005), a film set during the Persian Gulf War, and the musical Dreamgirls (2006).
