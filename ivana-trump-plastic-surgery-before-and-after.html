Ivana Trump Plastic Surgery: Before and After

Posted by Aldo Pusey on Monday, August 12, 2024

Ivana Trump was a Czech-American businesswoman, fashion designer, model, and media personality, who has been active in the media scenes for over five decades. Ivana Trump was known to many as the former wife of Donald Trump.

Ivana Trump was an alumnus of Charles University and she was the author of books like “Women Who Work” and “The Trump Card.”

Ivana Trump Plastic Surgery: Before and After

There has been several allegations and claims that Ivana Trump has undergone a plastic surgery but it has never been officially confirmed.

The beauty of Ivana Trump is very massive and some little transformation about her appearance made people presume these allegations of her plastic surgery.

Nevertheless, it is known to many that Ivana Trump’s daughter, Ivanka Trump, has underwent series of plastic surgeries that has transformed her massively.

Ivana Trump was born in Gottwaldov, Moravia, Czechoslovakia, on February 20, 1949, and died in New York City, US, on July 14, 2022, at the age of 73 years.
